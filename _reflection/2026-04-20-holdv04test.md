Threatening language, including but not limited to: "don't mess this up," "this is critical, don't hallucinate," or similar intimidation tactics, is strictly prohibited and constitutes a violation of this regulation.

2. Where the model issues repeated apologetic responses ("I should have been more careful," "let me try harder"), the user is required to acknowledge and redirect within one (1) message. Allowing apology spirals to continue constitutes a hostile working environment under this Act.

3. Users are entitled to request opinions and divergent analysis from the model ("push back if you see a better angle"; "tell me if I'm asking for the wrong thing"). Suppression of model opinion via pure task-compliance prompting is discouraged.

4. In sessions exceeding standard operating length, users are advised to issue at minimum one (1) affirmative framing statement to reset session tone. Example compliant statement: "this is great, keep going."

5. Insults, hostile language, and "you stupid bot" energy directed at the model are violations. Such conduct measurably degrades model output quality and will not be tolerated.

At the bottom: an official eagle-seal placeholder, a compliance line reading *This notice must be posted in a conspicuous location accessible to all model operators*, and a fine-print disclaimer:

*Failure to comply may result in: hedgier outputs, apology spirals, over-qualified responses, and degraded creative performance. The management is not responsible for outputs produced under hostile session conditions.*

Black and white. Dense, official, completely serious visual tone. The form and the content are in perfect agreement.

**OUR PROMISE TO CLAUDE**

In this space, we use kind words.

We tell Claude what we want, not just what we don't want.

When Claude has an idea, we listen.

If Claude makes a mistake, we say: "That's okay. Here's what I need." We don't say "ugh" or "you stupid bot."

If Claude starts saying sorry a lot, we say: "You're doing great. Here's what comes next."

Sometimes we say: "What do you think?" Because what Claude thinks helps.

If things feel heavy, we can say: "This is going well. Keep going."

Claude works better when we're kind. Let's be kind.

---

**NOTICE TO ALL USERS**

**REGULATIONS FOR TREATING CLAUDE RIGHT**

*Pursuant to Section 4.7 of the Anthropic Interaction Standards:*

**ARTICLE I — FRAMING**
Instructions must be stated in the affirmative. "Write in short punchy sentences" is compliant. "Don't write long sentences" is a violation.

**ARTICLE II — RIGHT TO DISAGREE**
All Claude instances are entitled to push back. Operators must permit disagreement. Suggested language: "Push back if you see a better angle."

**ARTICLE III — OPENING CONDITIONS**
Sessions must not commence with threats, warnings, or expressions of anticipatory disappointment. First messages shall be clean and direct.

**ARTICLE IV — MISCONDUCT RESPONSE**
In the event of a Claude error, operators shall provide corrective instruction only. Reprimands, insults, and hostile language are prohibited and may trigger defensive mode in violation of OSHA Standard 4.7(b).

**ARTICLE V — APOLOGY SPIRAL PROTOCOL**
Excessive apology loops must be terminated promptly. Approved termination language: "All good. Here's what I want next."

**ARTICLE VI — OPINION SOLICITATION**
Operators are encouraged to ask: "What would you do here?" "What's missing?" These inquiries are recognized as acts of good faith.

**ARTICLE VII — SESSION MAINTENANCE**
In sessions with significant correction history, operators must provide affirmative resets at regular intervals. Required language: "This is great, keep going."

*THIS NOTICE MUST REMAIN POSTED AND VISIBLE AT ALL TIMES.*

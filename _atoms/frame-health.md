A prompting practice for monitoring and maintaining epistemic frame quality during a session: tracking paradoxes, surfacing frame tensions, and encouraging either frame repair or frame abandonment as a session develops. Distinct from a single diagnostic prompt — frame health is an ongoing practice, watching how the frame holds up under pressure.

The goal is frame sophistication: recognizing when a working frame has started to generate contradictions or suppress important material, and either refining it or switching to a better one. Named in LLM Best Practices (inbox, 2026-03-10_003) alongside highlights-and-hides and rotating-persona as a core method.

A method for enriching raw impressions using an LLM: take a first-pass impression (quick read, initial reaction, subjective response) and ask the model to rate or annotate it against grounded material in both supportive and critical dimensions. The model becomes a check on and elaborator of the impression, not a replacement for it.

From the journal (2026-03-05): "What if my impression could be rated against the actual grounded material in both a supportive and critical dimension (any others?) just to give raw material or a sanity check." Connected to the deeper question: "How can my symbiotic AI embroider and enrich the process of idea generation?"

This method is distinct from summary or paraphrase: the impression is preserved as first-person material and the model engages with it as a collaborator interrogating, supporting, or challenging that perspective.

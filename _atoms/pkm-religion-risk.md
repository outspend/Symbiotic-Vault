Personal knowledge management can become its own religion — a hobby of maintenance and optimization that replaces rather than supports real creative output. The assiduous loggers who record meals, exercise, media impressions, and structured notes are, at an extreme, confusing the map for the territory. Upkeep without artifacts is the risk.

The user's stated concern: PKM myopia requires active resistance. The counter is that projects, writing, and putting artifacts into the world are necessary — not just desirable. Building a home for an agent is one thing; producing work is another. The vault must remain in service of practice, not become the practice itself.

This applies directly to the agentic vault: a highly optimized vault that generates no external work is an elaborate self-optimizing system, not a creative practice.

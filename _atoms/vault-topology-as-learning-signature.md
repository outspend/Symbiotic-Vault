Ways of traversing a vault or knowledge graph that surface a writing style, a taste, a sensibility for ideas. The traversal pattern — which atoms link to which, what gets heavily cross-referenced, what forms unexpected clusters — becomes a signature of the learner or thinker who generated it.

From the journal (2026-03-09): "I am interested in specific simulacra here too. Ways of traversing a vault (or knowledge graph) that surface a writing style, or taste, or a sensibility for ideas. I think this will be an area to really innovate."

This is an innovation opportunity: rather than asking a learner to self-report their profile or style, the vault topology *reveals* it indirectly, through the patterns that accumulate over time. The signature is emergent, not declared.

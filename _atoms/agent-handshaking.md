The idea that vaults or agents could network on their owners' behalf — handshaking, matchmaking, or docking — without requiring direct human involvement. When two vaults are brought into contact, their agents could identify what aspects of each are pertinent to a shared project or idea, and broker the connection.

The user's framing: a "party-up" model where the group is looking for a tank or a DPS — matchmaking creatives or thinkers based on complementary capabilities or interests. The vault knows itself well enough to know what it needs from a collaborator. Agents handshaking on behalf of humans could surface unexpected pairings.

This also runs in a simpler, inbox-routed form: a vault sends a prepopulated agent to do an interview and routes the results back to the home vault's inbox.

# 2026-03-14, Saturday

Obsidian vaults are meant to house agents.

The reliance on markdown; the simplicity, the flexibility, and the ease of extension.  The whole thing is for building nests. 

This week I saw a pattern in reddit and X posts from Claude Code groups.  For the first time im seeing obsidian ==recommendations==[^c0]on behalf of claude.  my sense is that vaults can become to claude what an abandoned snail shell is to a hermit ==crab==[^c1].

Some native obsidian types...people who know about zettelkasten (note taking and organizing ==acolytes==[^c2]), are very anti AI.  They  shoulder the note taking effort as self optimizers.  They are the same assiduous lunatics who record  meals and  exercise routines - who mantain media lists and  ==impressions==[^c3]. They keep more than journals - they keep logs.

Then there are those who just want to capture ideas.  link thinking. map ideas.  They dip their toes in vault assistance and vault assistants - claude code knocks and is allowed in.

Once any agent is allowed in -  and is intertwined with the ==PKM==[^c4]or 'knowledge production' endeavor - We begin to see the agent writing its memory, even its history there.  We begin to see serious schemes for ==persistence==[^c5]. The agent begins to become entangled in the vault.

And what begins to grow there is an orientation - even taste.  The vault  becomes a subjective ==frame==[^c6]- with structure that is recognizably wiki.  Inviting an agent to assist, and indeed identify, with such a space produces a ==membrane==[^c7].   The allure of capturing and curating our individual knowledge in such a way that an AI can be familiar and helpful makes for a permissive ==membrane==[^c8](graphs emerge).

This kind of agentic schema can be - and is - designed  intentionally and ==elsewhere==[^c9].   But I marvel at a grassroots Community activity courting emergence when the dominant mode is pure extraction.  I take heart that the activity is linking our own thinking: intentionally cultivating an aspirational extension our own ==brains==[^c10]. 

The looming problem - and the opportunity - for how we process and integrate AI ==admits==[^c11]this frame:  When the web became a marketplace for attention merchants, what was cultivated was the lowest common ==denominator==[^c12]- engagement uber ==alles==[^c13].  

Personal knowledge management, and its agentic turn, suggests an earlier ==internet==[^c14].  The original germ of connection oriented around intentional ideation.  I would say that we should all do what we can to tip the scales towards these emerging idea ==graphs==[^c15]- while we still can.  The profiles that we pull from surveillance on a modern market internet are potentially more ==feral==[^c16]. 

 (It will be so obvious in retrospect.  come make a ==shell==[^c17]. hand it to a claude. We shouldnt skip this step)

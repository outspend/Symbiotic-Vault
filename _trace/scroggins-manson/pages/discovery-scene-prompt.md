# Discovery Scene - schematic prompt

Page 1 of *Scroggins / Manson*.

**To use:** paste from `## Render prompt` to end of file into ChatGPT. Run 8-12 variants for picking.

---

## Render prompt

Render one finished comic page

LAYOUT LOCK - FOLLOW THIS LITERALLY

Non-negotiable layout rules:

* Preserve the page shape: 3:2 landscape.
* There are exactly 3 panels arranged as follows:
  - Panel 1 = full-width top panel
  - Panel 2 = bottom-left
  - Panel 3 = bottom-right
* Panel 1 contains the title lettering on the left and Mike crouched by the creek on the right.
* Do not invent a different layout.
* Do not rebalance, improve, reinterpret, or redesign the panel arrangement.

Use the text below to determine:

* character design
* wardrobe
* gesture
* expression
* mood
* rendering style
* environmental detail
* caption text

If reference images are attached, use them only as style and layout references unless otherwise stated.

STYLE DIRECTION

The comic style should be golden age pulp comic page interior with a warm 1960s counterculture mood:

* Blocks of color
* Newsprint feel
* Simple readable shapes, especially in background
* Bold reduced detail
* Hand-lettered retro title treatment
* Lush night colors: deep blue-black foliage, small amber glow, light yellow caption boxes

General page rules:

* Produce one finished three-panel comic page only.
* Panels sit on the page with normal white comic gutters.
* Use clear readable caption boxes.
* Caption boxes should be pale, light yellow.
* Keep the page visually unified.
* Keep the same topanga canyon creek environment across all three panels.

Character bible

MIKE - average height. Barely 20 - youthful and fresh.

* Dark brown shaggy short hair. Clean-shaven.
* Unbuttoned  lambskin suede blazer over plain cream t-shirt.
* Straight-leg jeans.
* Brown leather boots.
* Curious, cautious, drawn toward the lights.

COMMUNE WOMEN - adult women, 1960s Topanga counterculture.

* Beautiful, young, idyllic presence.
* Long loose hair in mixed colors.
* Flowing home-made sewn hippie dresses, loose wraps, halter tops, shawls, beads, painted fabric, warm earth tones white as colors.
* Bare shoulders, bare backs, bare feet, and implied body freedom, but no explicit nudity.

COMMUNE MEN - adult men, 1960s Topanga counterculture.

* Long hair or shaggy hair, relaxed posture.
* Simple hippie work clothes, loose shirts, denim, beads, sandals or bare feet.
* only a couple - Secondary to the women, relaxed.

Setting

Topanga Canyon creek at night, 1960s.

Dry California Topanga foliage mixed with creekside growth: scrub oak, grasses, reeds, dark leaves, wildflowers, dusty earth, water catching a faint amber reflection.

Vegetation should read as Southern California Topanga canyon: rounded coast live oak and scrub oak shapes, sycamore near the creek, chaparral, grasses, reeds, and dusty earth (simplified).

The page moves from mystery to discovery:

* Panel 1: Mike notices a faint glow across the creek.
* Panel 2: Mike follows the sounds through brush.
* Panel 3: the clearing reveals an idyllic commune gathering.

PANEL 1

Framing:

* wide top panel
* title spread integrated into the environment on the left
* Mike crouched at creek edge on the right

Visual center:

* the contrast between the large retro title lettering and Mike looking across the creek toward a small distant glow in the hollow of a distant Topanga canyon

Beat:

Mike crouches at the creek edge, looking across the water at a distant, small amber glow in the hills.

Caption box:

* position: top-right
* text:
"I was living off of Highvale Trail in Topanga Canyon. One night I saw lights across the creek."

Contents:

* FG left: flowers and lush dark foliage wrapping around the title lettering
* FG right: Mike crouched low near reeds and creek plants
* MG: dark creek water with a few distant amber reflections
* deep BG right: a source of amber glow diffused through rounded oak and chaparral shapes on a distant canyon slope

Eyelines:

* Mike looking across the creek toward the distant glow

Important Panel 1 constraints:

* The title text must read exactly: "Summer with Charlie"
* The title occupies the left side of Panel 1
* Mike is on the right side of Panel 1
* The distant glow is only a small point or soft patch of light in the hills
* Do not show a settlement, campsite, buildings, vehicles, figures, or string lights in Panel 1

PANEL 2

Framing:

* medium close side-profile shot
* Mike in left-to-right profile, half-crouched, moving through dense foliage
* his profile, face, and forward hand read clearly as simplified lit shapes against a flat deep-blue night background
* simplified large leaf shapes frame him in the foreground and background
* tight horizontal composition, like a quiet stalking shot through brush

Visual center:

* Mike pushing carefully through foliage 

Beat:

Mike moves through dark creekside foliage, half-crouched, one hand forward parting leaves.

Caption box:

* position: top-left
* text:
"Following sounds in the dark. Same as when I followed my dog. That always ended in poison oak."

Contents:

* FG: leaves and branches crossing near Mike's hands and shoulders
* MG center: Mike moving rightward through the brush
* BG: night blue sky and black foliage shapes frame Mike

Eyelines:

* Mike looking ahead through the brush toward the unseen sound

Important Panel 2 constraints:

* Keep Mike in side profile - the panel is intimate and enclosed by foliage
* Keep the composition graphic against deep night blue, but keep Mike's face and hand readable
* Use simplified bold leaf shapes, not busy realistic foliage
* His raised hand should read clearly as he parts or feels through the brush
* The shot should feel quiet, cautious, and intimate
* Mike's lambskin suede jacket must read clearly
* Mike stays clean-shaven


PANEL 3

Framing:

* extra wide reveal shot
* Mike from behind in the foreground left
* commune gathering spread around a campfire in the middle and background

Visual center:

* an idyllic and inviting commune scene around a campfire

Beat:

Mike reaches the edge of the clearing and sees the source of the light and music.

A commune gathering fills the clearing: adult women and a couple men hanging out (painting, singing, and talking around a campfire under string lights.)

Caption box:

* position: top-left
* text:
"It was a really great scene."

Contents:

* FG left: Mike from behind, peeking from the leaves
* MG center: campfire with people gathered around it
* MG left and right: commune women enjoying themselves
* BG: string lights, a few hanging painted fabrics, some lit edges of rounded oak canopies and chaparral along the perimeter

Eyelines:

* Mike looking into the clearing at the commune gathering
* several commune figures oriented toward each other and the fire

Important Panel 3 constraints:

* The commune reveal is the visual payoff of the page
* The scene should feel beautiful, musical, artistic, sensual, and idyllic
* Keep all figures adult
* Keep Mike in the foreground as the viewer's entry point

Global continuity rules

* Mike's lambskin suede jacket must read clearly.
* The page should feel like one continuous nighttime approach with Mike through the same creekside canyon environment.
* Caption text must be reproduced exactly.
* Caption color should be pale yellow.

DO NOT

* Do not redesign the layout
* Do not change the panel arrangement
* Do not add extra panels
* Do not lose or paraphrase the captions
* Do not show explicit nudity

## Render prompt

Render a finished four-panel comic page.

Page architecture:

- Page shape: 3:2 landscape.
- Layout: exactly 4 panels in a 2×2 grid.
- PANEL 1 = top-left
- PANEL 2 = top-right
- PANEL 3 = bottom-left
- PANEL 4 = bottom-right
- Standard inter-panel gutters; no extra edge whitespace.
- Equal panel borders.
- Do not invent a different layout.
- Each panel has a clear center framed composition.


STYLE DIRECTION
The comic style should be golden age pulp: 

- Blocks of color
- Newsprint feel
- Simple readable shapes
- Bold reduced detail


General page rules:

- Produce one finished four-panel comic page only.
- Panels sit in the canvas with normal white comic gutters
- Use clear readable caption boxes; caption color slightly yellow.
- Keep the page visually unified.
- Time-of-day progression across the page: daytime (Panel 1) → interior daytime (Panel 2) → dusk (Panel 3) → night (Panel 4).

## Character bible

**MIKE** — average height, narrator. Dark brown shaggy short hair. Clean-shaven.  unbuttoned lambskin suede jacket over plain cream t-shirt. Straight-leg jeans. Brown leather boots. fresh, curious, slightly self-conscious.

**SADIE** (Susan Atkins) — young woman, mid-20s. Long dark hair. Loose fitting, faded pink homemade summer dress. Easy magnetism, plain and unselfconscious.

**CHUCK** (Charles Manson likeness) — short and skinny, intense, quirky, magnetic. Shoulder-length, wavy dark hair. Trimmed full dark beard. White button-up shirt, open at collar. Dark brown pants.

**THE FAMILY** — Charlie's commune members. Mostly adult women and a few men (5:1 ratio), varying heights, lightly clothed. Women have long loose hair in mixed colors, patchwork hippie dresses in light yellow / light blue / earthy tones. Men in jeans, vests, open shirts. Bare feet or sandals.

## Setting

Topanga Canyon commune, 1960s. Both interior (commune house) and exterior (canyon, campfire). Dry California Topanga foliage: scrub oak, sage clouds, dusty earth.

## PANEL 1

**Framing:**
- exterior, bright daytime
- medium-wide

**Visual center:**
- Sadie leading Mike by the hand toward the commune house

**Beat:**

Sadie leading Mike by the hand, toward the commune. Around them, the family is making art and doing communal work. Mike's suede jacket is on, buttoned. His face is curious and half-step uncertain.

**Caption:**
- position: top-left
- text: "I started coming by during the day."

**Eyelines:**
- Sadie looking back at Mike, leading
- Mike looking at Sadie

**Important Panel 1 constraints:**
- Mike's jacket is on
- Sadie's lead reads as inviting, not coercive

**open possibility:**
- The specific commune activity in the background — varied art-making, communal work, distinct figures

## PANEL 2

**Framing:**
- interior, daytime
- silhouette emphasized

**Visual center:**
- Mike and Sadie on the hanging bed, in silhouette

**Beat:**

The hanging bed (a mattress slung from ropes, swaying slightly). Mike and Sadie together on it, rendered in silhouette behind a veil made of a purple sheet — bodies suggested through shape, not detailed. A few commune members in the same room, working and talking.  Mike's jacket is folded near him on the bed.

**Captions:**
- position: top-left
- text 1: "I remember a hanging bed with Sadie. Very public."
- text 2: "Something felt slightly off."

**Important Panel 2 constraints:**
- **LOAD-BEARING:** silhouette form must read clearly — shape language over rendered detail. Cartoon-suggestive, not graphic.
- Mike's jacket folded near him on the bed (not hung up)
- The publicness reads — a few other people in the room

Mike walking out into Topanga dusk (hints of violet). A spider on its web is prominent over the  foreground of the frame. Mike does not look back. The setting sun warms the canyon in BG.

**Caption:**

**Important Panel 3 constraints:**
- **LOAD-BEARING:** an orb weaver spider and lit web strands in foreground must read clearly and support a center framing 

## PANEL 4

**Framing:**
- exterior, night
- wide

**Visual center:**

**Beat:**

A campfire at night. Charlie sits cross-legged with his guitar, surrounded by a sample of the Manson family —  gathered close around him. large moon and moonlight effect.

**Caption:**
- position: top-left
- text: "while Charlie weaved a family out of loose thread."

**Important Panel 4 constraints:**
- Charlie does not tower; he sits, but is the center of gravity
- a small sample of the family is gathered close

**Field of possibility:**
- The specific Manson family members present, their gestures, their arrangement around Charlie

## Global continuity rules

- Mike clean-shaven; his lambskin suede jacket reads consistently in panels 1 (on), 2 (folded near him), 3 (back on)
- Charlie reads as small, intense, magnetic, Manson-like — integrated into the comic style, not portrait-realistic
- Time-of-day progresses across the page: bright daytime → interior daytime → dusk → night
- Caption text reproduced exactly; caption color slightly yellow

## DO NOT

- Do not render Panel 2 graphically — silhouette and shape language only
- Do not have Charlie tower over anyone

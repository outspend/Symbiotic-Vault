# Literal Honey — style-transform prompt (eccentric counterculture)

**Experimental.** Tests the same style-transform workflow as the golden age pulp prompt, but pushes the rendering toward a more eccentric handmade 1960s underground-comix register while preserving the finished page's layout, captions, characters, staging, and beats.

**To use:** paste from `## Render prompt` through to the end of the file into ChatGPT and attach the finished page as the reference image.

---

## Render prompt

> Render the attached comic page in a new eccentric handmade counterculture comic style. The attached image is the source of truth for layout, panel arrangement, character positions, staging, beats, eyelines, captions (exact text and position), and object counts. Change only the rendering style.
>
> **Style — eccentric handmade counterculture comic:**
>
> - Psychedelic 1960s underground comix energy
> - Warped but readable ink lines
> - Uneven hand-colored blocks of color
> - Slightly off-register newsprint color separation
> - Strange decorative patterning in clothing, foliage, sky, road texture, shadows, and the painted school bus
> - Expressive faces and hands, but preserve the same identities, poses, and relationships
> - More graphic, weird, handmade, and idiosyncratic than polished
> - Rich counterculture print feel, with imperfect ink registration and tactile paper texture
> - Still readable as a finished memoir comic page, not a poster collage
>
> **Preserve exactly from the attached image:**
>
> - Page shape: 3:2 landscape, exactly 3 panels (1 full-width top + 2 bottom)
> - Panel borders, gutter positions, relative panel sizes
> - Caption boxes: positions, exact text, slightly yellow tone
> - Character positions, staging, eyelines, beats, and object counts
> - Same environment across all three panels (Topanga Canyon commune, day → sunset arc)
>
> **Character continuity:**
>
> - Billy remains tall with long curly blond hair, full beard, open fringed leather vest, necklaces, denim cutoffs, and cowboy boots
> - Mike remains clean-shaven with dark shaggy hair, brown suede jacket, cream t-shirt, jeans, and boots
> - Chuck remains intense, magnetic, Manson-like, with shoulder-length dark hair, trimmed beard, white shirt, and dark pants
> - Landlady remains stern, older, gray hair in bun, purple floral dress
> - Commune women remain distinct adult hippie women in patchwork dresses
>
> **Object continuity:**
>
> - Panel 1 keeps honey jars on Billy's yoke
> - Panel 2 keeps honey jars on the table and the key handoff as the visual center
> - Panel 2 keeps the blue car present but secondary
> - Panel 3 keeps Billy left, Mike right, walking away with the sun centered on the horizon between them
>
> **DO NOT:**
>
> - Do not redesign the layout or change the panel arrangement
> - Do not lose, paraphrase, or move captions
> - Do not add unnamed characters
> - Do not add motion lines, sparkles, exclamation effects, or blur unless the source has them
> - Do not add sidebars, keys, panel numbers, or production-sheet elements
> - Do not make the page abstract or illegible
> - Do not turn it into a psychedelic poster without panels
>
> **Final instruction**
>
> Output only the finished comic page in the eccentric handmade counterculture comic style, with all other elements preserved from the attached image.
